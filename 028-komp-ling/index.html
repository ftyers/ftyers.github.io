Marking

  • 40% Quizzes
  • 50% Hackathon
  • 10% Active participation

Quizzes: Most classes there will be a quiz for homework, this will be due the Thursday after the class at 21:00. For example the quiz for the 11th September will be due on the 14th September. Each quiz will consist of approximately 5—10 questions with equal weight towards the final mark. The URL to the Google form where you can fill in the quiz will be linked from this page.

Hackathon: TBD

Active participation: Beyond simply showing up, we encourage you to contribute to discussions by asking questions, answering questions, making relevant comments, helping classmates and asking for help with in-class activities, etc. There are no stupid questions — we want to make sure everyone grasps the concepts, and many are not as straightforward as they may first seem (or as we think they are). You are also expected to have read any assigned readings before class.

In the classroom

Note on pronouns: If you'd like to be referred to by a pronoun that you think we might not guess correctly or if you notice us referring to you by some other pronoun than what you'd prefer, please let us know so that we can get it right.

Note on language: Please feel free to communicate in with the lecturers and each other in any language you feel comfortable with. If we are talking too fast, or use expressions that you do not understand and you think it is having an effect on your learning, please ask us to slow down or explain. You are welcome to come and talk to us at any time about issues relating to language. We will be happy to discuss them.
